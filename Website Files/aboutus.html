ABOUT Us

The main goal of this project is to create a restaurant management software system which is expected to save time both for customers and the restaurant management team. This Restaurant Management System DBMS will help to run the business more efficiently. With this system, restaurant management can maintain staff information, schedule, supplier information, payroll, reservations and most importantly, customers can order food online with a copy of it being sent to the kitchen and cash counter immediately.
